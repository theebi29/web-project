About This Class

Our Boxing classes combine traditional boxing techniques with modern fitness principles to deliver a full-body workout that builds strength, endurance, and confidence. Suitable for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced boxers.

Technical Training

Learn proper stance, footwork, and punching techniques including jabs, crosses, hooks, and uppercuts.

Bag Work

Develop power and precision with heavy bag, speed bag, and double-end bag training.

Conditioning

High-intensity interval training with jump rope, shadow boxing, and bodyweight exercises.

Defense Drills

Master blocking, slipping, and counter-punching techniques.
